Latest virus: Covid-19

Covid 19 is the latest virus that everyone has heard of, forcing some people into lockdown until it is safe to come outside for long periods of time.

Covid 19 is a “Crown-like spike” that latches onto and prys into living cells. The best way to stay safe from covid is to prevent it from getting into your body in the first place. After the virus multiplies, it will start moving down the body. After a few days, the immune system should come to fight the virus. If the immune system is unsuccessful in killing the virus, Covid 19 will become deadly as it enters the lungs. The reason it can be so deadly is because it attacks ACE-2 rich cells that are in the air sacs of the lungs. Which in turn will reduce the patient's oxygen levels.


Symptoms:

Common symptoms:

  • Dry Cough
  • Fever
  • Fatigue

Less Common symptoms:

  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Sore throat
  • Pink eye
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Skin rash
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

Serious Symptoms:

  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of speech or movement

Latest Variant: Omicron

Though Omicron is not as the previous Variant of Covid(Delta), it is highly contagious. The cases have increased to almost 3 times more than from April 2021. 1 case unreported of Omicron can lead to an entire school or facility shut down.
